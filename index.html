How to Work From Home and Get Stuff Done

An ever-increasing number of contractors and full-time employees are working from home — or daydreaming about working from home.

Create Some Space

Designate a workspace at home for those late nights. This can be anywhere you have access to a flat surface and adequate lighting that allows you to concentrate — a desk, your kitchen table, a reading chair, just not your bed! This helps your mind transition to “home” mode when you’re done.
